About me

Companies

Engr Soji Adeniji is the Managing director of OAT Construction Limited a Leading Construction company in Nigeria, with a focus on High-end Real Estate and Infrastructural Development, he spearheads diverse projects including Industrial/Commercial buildings, specialized petroleum downstream projects, and mixed-use residential developments across Nigeria.

Adesoji Aadeniji is also the CEO & Founder of Mikunki Investment Limited a registered leading international export- import with the focus of exporting raw and dried split ginger, garlic and turmeric, palm oil, palm kernel oil, dried plantain and plantain flour tamarind and other required agricultural produce to any country of your choice.

He is also the CEO & Founder of Soji Adeniji Consultancy, a leading consultancy based in Lagos Nigeria where he helps business scale and grow their enterpirse with his years of experience, skills and Education.

Certifications

With over twenty-two years of experience, Soji holds leadership roles in strategic construction economics, administrative matters, and project delivery. As a Fellow of the Institute of Management Specialists (F.I.M.S.) and the Nigerian Institute of Quantity Surveyors (FNIQS), he brings expertise in Quantity Surveying and Project Management.

Education

Soji Adeniji obtained his HND in Quantity Surveying from The Polytechnic Ibadan in 1990, followed by a Postgraduate Diploma from the University of Calabar and a Masters in Project Management from the University of Lagos in 2000. His academic journey began at Igbo-Ora, Oyo State, and Government College Ibadan, where he completed his O/Level and A/Level certificates respectively.

Experience in the Workspace

Prior to Founding OAT Construction Nig. Ltd, Soji worked diligently with Adold Engineering Co. Ltd and Projectscope Ltd.
