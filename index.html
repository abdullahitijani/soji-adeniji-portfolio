10 Years Experience

Adesoji Aadeniji is also the CEO & Founder of Mikunki Investment Limited a registered leading international export- import with the focus of exporting raw and dried split ginger, garlic and turmeric, palm oil, palm kernel oil, dried plantain and plantain flour tamarind and other required agricultural produce to any country of your choice.
